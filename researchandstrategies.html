Research & Strategies

There are many different ways to get ideas, CNBC news, fellow traders, family and friends. But how reliable are these sources.


RFA research team, screens for companies that meet a customized set of criteria, we generate investment ideas, create lists of securities to follow, and validate the ideas by seeing how they performed historically. The team create criteria based on categories such as sectors, industries, geographies and asset classes, as well as create criteria based on data fields including fundamentals, estimates, and financial ratios. We analyze growth, trends, and estimate revisions quarterly, as well as compare data against benchmarks or relative data.


Sector based research, we analyze market worldwide for investment ideas, our research analysis uses a variety of methodologies and sources including research reports, industry outlook and public documents.


The long-term view is a cornerstone of our approach. It provides a summary of our investment views on a stock, and including scenario analysis, risk, catalysts and peer comparison.
